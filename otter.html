Mr. Otter

"Otters gonna ott." - Mr. Otter

Mr. Otter is a sea otter. He lives in a london flat with six roomates; four otters and two humans. He loves wearing crowns, receiving fan mail, snuggling with his friends in a large pile and telling stories about his life on the sea. He is very attached to his orange starfish, his favorite color is green, and his favorite animal besides his starfish or a yummy clam is an ottercorn.

These are my Otter Roomates!
