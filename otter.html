Mr. Otter

"Otters gonna ott." - Mr. Otter

Mr. Otter is a sea otter. He lives in a london flat with ten roomates! (London is quite expensive so flat sharing is essential) His roomates include: five otters; two humans; a rat; a baby squirrel; and an angry llama.

Some of Mr. Otters favorite activities are wearing crowns, receiving fan mail, snuggling with his friends in a large pile, and telling stories about his life on the sea. He is very attached to his orange starfish. His favorite color is green, and his favorite animals are his starfish, a yummy clam, and the majestic ottercorn!
